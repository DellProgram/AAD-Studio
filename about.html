Our Story

Vision, Passion, and Excellence Since 2006.

The Visionary Behind the Lens

Above All Dreams was founded in 2006 by Benard Cohen II, a visionary with a passion for capturing moments and telling stories. With nearly two decades of experience, Benard has cultivated a studio that stands for creativity, professionalism, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

A Legacy of Diverse Projects

Our portfolio is a testament to our versatility and expertise. We have had the privilege of working on a vast array of projects, from managing websites for businesses of all sizes to creating compelling visual content for esteemed organizations like Jones High School and Zeta Alpha Psi. Each project is a partnership, where we work tirelessly to bring our client's vision to life.

Our Mission

At Above All Dreams, our mission is simple: to provide the best possible service to every client looking to expand their brand and share their story.
